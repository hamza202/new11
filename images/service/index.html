About biolab arabia

BioLab Arabia (BLA) produces and supplies under license from BWA Water Additives, UK antiscalant chemicals for both thermal and reverse osmosis desalination plants for the effective solutions for today's water treatment needs.

BLA is exclusively devoted to manufacture the antiscalants for the water treatment sector in Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and dedicated to provide services to customers all over GCC with the essential elements required to meet today's water management challenges.

Our brands; Belgard® and Flocon® are comprehensive solutions for thermal and RO operations and recognized worldwide for the highest possible quality and superior technical performance.

BLA products fully comply with all relevant regulatory approvals.

BioLab Arabia is a private limited company registered in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and located at Assistance Industries Area, Jubail Industrial City, Road # 106, Jubail, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
